Incoterms

Trade Terms We Support

Flexible international shipping arrangements tailored to your operations.

EXW

Ex Works

Buyer arranges transportation, freight, insurance, and export handling. Goods are collected directly from the supplier's facility.

FOB

Free On Board

Seller delivers goods to the port and loads them onto the vessel. Buyer handles freight and insurance after shipment.

CIF

Cost, Insurance & Freight

Seller manages transportation, freight charges, insurance, and export documentation until goods reach the destination port.
