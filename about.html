Who We Are

About West Logistic Scutis Service LLC.

West Logistic Scutis Service LLC is an international commodity export and logistics company specializing in premium-quality palm oil supply and global trade solutions.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between producers and international buyers by providing reliable sourcing, quality assurance, export documentation, freight coordination, and timely delivery.

We work closely with trusted producers and logistics partners to ensure every shipment meets international standards and arrives safely at its destination.

Through transparency, professionalism, and operational excellence, we have built strong relationships with buyers across multiple continents — whether supplying bulk quantities for industrial processing, food manufacturing, wholesale distribution, or commercial use.

To become a globally recognized leader in palm oil exports and logistics solutions by providing world-class service, sustainable trade practices, and long-term value for our clients.
